Tyler McDaniel is a filmmaker and videographer based in Louisville, KY. His style is influenced by a working class ethos that draws from the nurture of Appalachia's foothills, New York City's bustling streets, the muddy Ohio River's gentle flow, and the eclectic cultures that they breed.

Apart from producing his own projects Tyler is also available on a freelance basis for music videos, promotional/informational videos, documentaries, commercials, and films. Please email for video or crew rates.
